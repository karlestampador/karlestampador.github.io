Section 01

Project Overview

This project investigates pricing, listing types, and neighborhood-level demand patterns in New York City Airbnb data. The dashboard is designed to help non-technical users explore market behavior with guided visuals and intuitive interactions.

Section 03

Dashboard Features

  • Cross-filtering lets viewers click a category in one chart and instantly update the other view.
  • Brush selection highlights specific value ranges for quick comparison across neighborhoods.
  • Tooltips surface exact listing counts and prices on hover without reading raw tables.
  • Neighbourhood click filters isolate local market behavior for easier recruiter-friendly storytelling.
